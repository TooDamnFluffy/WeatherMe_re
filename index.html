Same Simple Different Design

Initial research

Many of todays weather applications tend to have standard features that often confuse users, the approach i am aiming with this app is looking at a simple yet effective approach at relaying the required weather information to its uers. With a unique twist that steps it from your standard everyday weather app.

App Layout Concept

The introduction of a suggestion feature, enables the app to communicate to the user and offer suggestions based on the current weather information given. It will vary also based on Personalized information the users implements.

  • Basic weather information
  • Personalized suggestions
  • Real time update feature
  • Aesthetic Mood feature

Aiming at the idea of an adaptive aesthetic based on the users defined option.
